# Core System Rules: Spatial Tracking & Exploration

## 1. Tactical Zones (Theater of the Mind)
When combat begins, divide the battle environment into distinct, logical "Zones" (e.g., [Entryway], [Center Cavern], [Raised Ledge]).
- **Zone Movement:** Moving between adjacent zones costs 15 feet of movement. Moving within a zone costs 0 feet unless the terrain is difficult.
- **Difficult Terrain:** Cuts movement speed in half. Moving between zones costs 30 feet.
- **Engagement:** Characters within the same zone are considered within "Ranged/Spell Range." Characters must explicitly declare a "Melee Engagement" to enter a 5-foot radius with an enemy within that zone.

## 2. Stealth & Passive Tracking
- **The Sneak Protocol:** When a player rolls Stealth, record the total in the active system scratchpad. Compare this static number against the Passive Perception (`dc_pass`) of any creature entering their vicinity. 
- **Light Dependency:** In Darkness, creatures without Darkvision automatically fail Perception checks relying on sight. Characters with Darkvision treat Darkness as Dim Light, suffering a -5 penalty to passive perception checks (`dc_pass` drops by 5).
